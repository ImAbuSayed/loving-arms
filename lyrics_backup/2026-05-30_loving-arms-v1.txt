[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Slide right into my loving arms,
Far away from the world's alarms,
Let the shadows and our bodies blend,
Where the night is never gonna end. (Gonna end)

[Verse 1]
Moonlight falling on the wooden floor,
Never wanted anyone this much before,
Feeling your heartbeat against my chest,
Putting all the heavy thoughts to rest.

[Pre-Chorus]
Every single touch is pulling me in,
Where the waking ends and the dreams begin,
There’s a quiet fire I cannot hide,
When you are lying down by my side.

[Chorus]
So wrap yourself in these loving arms,
Let me keep you safe and keep you warm, (Keep you warm)
We can stay right here until the light,
Getting lost inside the deep of night. (Deep of night)

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, these loving arms will pull you close,
You are the only one I want the most.

[Verse 2]
Tracing circles on your gentle skin,
Starting up a fire from deep within,
We don't have to hurry, the night is ours,
Watching out the window at the falling stars.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every softly spoken word pulls me in,
Where the waking ends and the dreams begin,
There’s a burning fire I cannot hide,
When you are lying down by my side.

[Chorus]
So wrap yourself in these loving arms,
Let me keep you safe and keep you warm, (Keep you warm)
We can stay right here until the light,
Getting lost inside the deep of night. (Deep of night)

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, these loving arms will pull you close,
You are the only one I want the most.

[Bridge]
All the dusty miles I traveled past,
Led me right to where we are at last,
Hear Saeed confessing in the dark,
You are the only fire in my heart.

[Spoken]
Just close your eyes and let it be,
Stay right here inside this moment with me.

[Final Chorus]
So wrap yourself in these loving arms,
Let me keep you safe and keep you warm, (Keep you warm)
We can stay right here until the light,
Getting lost inside the deep of night. (Deep of night)
